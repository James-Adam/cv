About Me

Since receiving my first ZX Spectrum when I was 6 I have wanted to build and create things with computers - and have been doing so ever since. I have built software for desktop and mobile as well as full stack web applications, from 2D adventure games to homework portals with 5 million users. I am forever learning and love the challenge of new technology, producing something special - especially if it entertains my 2 year old son.
